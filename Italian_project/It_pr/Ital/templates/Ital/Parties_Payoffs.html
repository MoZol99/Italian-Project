Gain for ALPHA and BETA party members:

- Assume you have chosen the VOTE option.

o If your party has more members who chose to vote than the other party then you will earn 105 points in this period.

o If both parties have the same number of members who have chosen to vote then your gain for this period will be 55 points.

o If, finally, your party has fewer members who chose to vote than the other party then you will gain 5 points for this period.

- Now suppose you have chosen option Y.

o If your party has more members who chose to vote than the other party then you will earn 105 points plus your Y bonus in this period.

o If both parties have the same number of members who have chosen to vote then your gain for this period will be 55 points plus your Y bonus.

o If your party has fewer members who have chosen to vote than the other party then you will earn 5 points plus your Y bonus for this period.

The amount of bonus Y for each ALPHA and BETA party member is randomly assigned by the computer at the beginning of each period. In each period of this session each ALPHA and BETA party member will have the same probability of being awarded a bonus Y ranging from 0 to 55.

The bonus Y that will be awarded to each member of the ALPHA and BETA party depends neither on the value of the bonus Y that was awarded in the previous periods nor on the decisions made in the previous periods. Similarly, the value of the Y bonus that you will be awarded does not depend on the value of the Y bonus of the other members nor on their choices. 

Y bonuses are allocated separately to each component so each participant's Y bonus is typically different from the others. Each member will know the value of their own bonus Y but not that of the others.
